Morphing Matter

The Morphing Matter lab develops materials, tools, and applications of adaptive, dynamic and intelligent morphing matter from nano to macro scales. We turn fictional futures into the present.

Currently I focus on shape memory fibers, specifically fully reversible thread actuators, and the fabrication methods of such fibers at longer than ever made before lengths. My research interests lie in the fabrication and application of new and interesting methods of actuation.


Earlier this year, our team's work was featured in Lunar Gala, a student run fashion show at Carnegie Mellon. The above video highlights our research.
