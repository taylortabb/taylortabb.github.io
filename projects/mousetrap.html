Mousetrap Car Challenge

In my Fundamentals of Mechanical Engineering class (Spring 2015), I and three other team members designed and built a small mousetrap propelled vehicle with the challenge of creating the lightest weight vehicle capable of traveling the furthest distance while maintain certain design and material restrictions. Through group collaboration, our team won the competition with a novel and aesthetically pleasing design incorporating a 3D printed pulley and belt system very small and very low friction ball bearings.
