Projects

In Professor Christopher Atkeson's Humanoids Robotics course, my group fabricated a dexterous glove actuator that could ameliorate the process of gripping objects for those with limited grip strength.

This past summer I built a radio telescope to observe the Hydrogen Line.

I'm also the head mechanic for SigEp Racing, a composite gravity racer team that's a part of CMU's Buggy program.

In Spring of 2015, my team won our Fundamentals Of Mechanical Engineering mousetrap car challenge and also designed and fabricated a wrench from an aluminum bar.

I've taken a number of courses on digital tooling and fabrication, and this Laser Cut Clock & Paper Lamp show some fun things I've done with laser cutting.
