MORPHING MATTER

The Morphing Matter Lab at CMU's School of Computer Science, develops materials, tools, and applications of adaptive, dynamic and intelligent morphing matter from nano to macro scales. We turn fictional futures into the present. My current work is on MODIFIBER, a fully reversible thread actuator for tangible interaction.

As a part of The Human Computer Interaction Institute, a large focus of this research is in enabling new venues for tacticle experiances. The applications of MODIFIBER thread actuators range from greeting cards to functional garments and cooking; in many areas where linear or rotational actuation was previosly challenging, thread actuators like MODIFIBER can create new tactile experiances.
