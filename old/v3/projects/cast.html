DIY CAST PART: Bobbin Holder

Overview
Initially, I wanted to cast two interconneted loops. That turned out to be a little out of the scope of the project, so the part used is a part I use to hold and spin a thread bobbin in a research project I am involved with. Currently, it is a part that breaks often and needs replacing, so I thought it'd be useful to make my DIY part have a useful secondary function as component of a research project! The wall thickness is approximately 1/16th of an inch at its minimum, and the parting line is straight along the center of the part lengthwise.
